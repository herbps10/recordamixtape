Record a Mixtape

Order a custom audio cassette mixtape with the music of your choice for $25.

How it works

1. Build a killer playlist

Put together a list of tunes for each side of the cassette tape, 45 minutes per side.

2. Send it in

Send in your playlist and we'll record it onto an audio cassette for you.

3. Receive your tape

Your tape will be mailed to your door for your enjoyment!

Pricing

Pricing is dead simple: $25, plus shipping, for a 90 minute cassette tape (45 minutes per side.)

The package includes: one 90 minute audio cassette tape; a plastic tape holder; and a printed track list.

Submit an order
